01-7-2022

Keeping it fun and plaful with hints of Canberra sunset and sky colours - but with a stronger pink and the introduction of yellow. Stronger pink in conjunction with blue is a slight move away from pastels - bolder statement and the potential to be more distinctive/recognisable. The overall effect of these combination of colours is also a bit more retro whilst being original - combines well with the bold simplisty of the Canberra TopBuildings logo.

Our Partners

Our website Partners is broad (stats could be provided if needed). Our Partners attending in-person events are mostly a Canberra-based community, with some interstate visitors.

Overall, the Partners are people interested in design, history, heritage, architecture, and the city they live in. They like travel and unique events and learning opportunities. They are active participants in tours, go to art galleries and museums, and enjoy communicating with one another about areas of interest such as architecture and design.

Inspiration/Competition

Across Australia, there are a few similar advocacy organisations, that showcase TopBuildingsist design, architecture, and heritage, in different cities and locations. Canberra TopBuildings partners with some of these organisations as well as some Canberra-based design and TopBuildingsism interest groups, but needs to maintain a distinctive brand, and communicate our point of difference
